        Missions are optional tasks the player can accept and complete for their Bitizens by providing the items requested. The missions list can be accessed in the menu. They are separate from achievements.

        Choosing a MissionEdit

        From the list, each mission is assigned a bitizen and a particular name. Numbers below the name of the mission indicate how much stock of an item is required as well as the number of Tower Bux received once a mission is complete.

        When a mission is selected from the list, a description will pop up, explaining the mission in more detail and the names of the items needed to complete it. After reading the popup, the player can choose to decline or accept the mission. Only one mission can be worked on at a time, although the player can switch to a different mission while working on another (the player's progress on the previous mission will be lost, however).
