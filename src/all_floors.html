        List of All Floors

        Edit
        • Classic editor
        • History
        Comments290
        227pages on
        this wiki

        Floors are the main components of Tiny Tower. They all have unique themes and names. Commercial floors (Food, Service, Recreation, Retail, and Creative) sell a variety of merchandise while Residential floors, also known as apartments, house Bitizens. Below is a visual list of all floors.
